Subject line formula: 
[Trigger Word / Hook] + [Value / Benefit] + [Curiosity / Time Element]
Pro tip:
Keep it under 9 words
Always hint at what’s inside, not everything
----
Email writing formula: AIDA formula
A – Attention → Start with a short, relatable hook.
I – Interest → Show what’s in it for them.
D – Desire → Add a benefit, success, or opportunity.
A – Action → End with 1 clear next step.
